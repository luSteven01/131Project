Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our service.

Booking Process

How do I book a flight for my pet?

You can book a flight for your pet by using our online booking system. Simply select your departure and arrival locations, choose your flight, and provide the required information about your pet.

Can I book a flight for multiple pets at once?

Yes, you can book flights for multiple pets in one transaction. Just provide the details for each pet during the booking process.

What information do I need to provide when booking a flight for my pet?

You will need to provide your pet's name, breed, weight, and any special requirements they may have. You will also need to provide your contact information and emergency contact details.

Payment and Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. Your payment details are securely processed through our payment gateway.

What is your refund policy?

If you need to cancel your booking, you may be eligible for a refund depending on the terms and conditions of your booking. Please contact our customer service for more information.

How can I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact our customer service team with your booking details and reason for the refund request.

Travel Requirements

What are the travel requirements for pets?

All pets must have a valid health certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian. The certificate should be issued within 10 days of travel. Please check our travel requirements page for more details.

Are there any breed or size restrictions for pets?

Some airlines have breed and size restrictions for pets. Please check with your airline for specific requirements before booking.

Do I need to provide any health certificates for my pet?

Yes, you will need to provide a health certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian. The certificate should be issued within 10 days of travel.

On the Flight

How should I prepare my pet for the flight?

Ensure your pet is well-rested and has had a chance to relieve themselves before the flight. Provide a comfortable and secure carrier for your pet.

What should I pack for my pet's flight?

Pack a small bag with your pet's essentials, including food, water, a leash, and any medications they may need.

Where will my pet be placed during the flight?

Your pet will be placed in a secure and climate-controlled area of the aircraft. Small pets may be allowed to travel in the cabin with you.

Can I visit my pet during the flight?

Unfortunately, you will not be able to visit your pet during the flight. However, rest assured that our staff will ensure your pet's safety and comfort.

After Booking

How can I change or cancel my booking?

You can change or cancel your booking by logging into your account and accessing your booking details. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.

How can I check the status of my booking?

You can check the status of your booking by logging into your account and accessing your booking details.

What if my pet gets sick before the flight?

If your pet gets sick before the flight, please contact your veterinarian and our customer service team as soon as possible.

Miscellaneous

Do you offer frequent flyer programs for pets?

Yes, we offer a frequent flyer program for pets. Please check our frequent flyer page for more details.

Can I earn miles or points for booking pet flights?

Yes, you can earn miles or points for booking pet flights. Please check our frequent flyer page for more details.

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

If you have more questions, please contact our customer service team. We are here to help!
